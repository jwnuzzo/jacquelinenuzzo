Framed Artworks

These are a selection of various framed artworks I made while I was the Lead Digital Artist and Content Manager at Grand Image from 2020-2022. They are a mix of final art and concept art made for both clients and Grand Image Home, an extension of Grand Image.

All works were created by me unless otherwise stated, under the art direction of Kate Maass, Art Director at Grand Image.
