ARTWORK RETOUCHING


These are a few examples of artwork I was responsible for digitally retouching as the Lead Digital Artist and Content Manager at Grand Image. This involved adding paper textures, cleaning up lines and accidental marks, and adding drop shadows to scans of painted works before the reprinting and editioning of each artwork. This series of works was made by artist Dana Gibson.

This artowrk was made as the Lead Digital Artist and Content Manager at Grand Image from 2020-2022 under the art direction of Kate Maass, Art Director at Grand Image.
