NBC MIDTERM ELECTION


I developed the art direction for NBC New's coverage of the 2022 midterm elections. This involved creating the risograph-style treatment and texture that was used across all graphics, as well as selecting accent colors to be used alongside NBC's official branding. I produced several media graphics that were used on NBC's landing page, live blog, social media page banners, Instagram, and Meet the Press.

These were made during my internship with NBC News from September-December 2022 under the art direction of Chelsea Stahl, Art Director of NBC News and MSNBC Digital.
