BRIDGEWATER COMMONS

This project was made for the Bridgwater Commons shopping mall in Bridgewater Township, New Jersey. Our client wanted a floral themed ceiling wallcovering that oversaw the entirety of the mall. As Grand Image's Lead Digital Artist and Content Manager, I oversaw the production of this wallcovering and produced the artwork myself. The client wanted to use components from paintings by artist, Kate Roebuck. I selected various artworks from Roebuck that fit the client's aesthetic preferences, digitally isolated them, recolored them to match our client's brand guidelines, added patterns and textures, and produced a unique composition.
